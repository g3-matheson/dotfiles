2) intercept-build:
  ships with LLVM, similar to bear:
  use: intercept-build scons

One Mac-specific note: bear on macOS uses DYLD_INSERT_LIBRARIES to intercept compiler calls, which can be blocked by System Integrity Protection (SIP) in some cases. If bear produces an empty or incomplete compile_commands.json, that's likely why — scons-compiledb would be the fallback.
